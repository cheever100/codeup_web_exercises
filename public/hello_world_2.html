Hello Class!

My name is Jean Cheever. I'm sorry I did not get to meet everyone the first morning of class. I was born and raised in San Antonio but spent about 20 years working on the East Coast primarily in the Pharma industry. I have been producing theatre for the past 13 years in New York City.
Although I split my time 50/50 between San Antonio and New York City, lately I've been in San Antonio more. I have gotten involved in the biotech world and am currently a board member for StemBioSys, a stem cell startup commercializing technology from the UTHSC. I am also on the board of a local bank and an active member of the Mayor's Fitness Council. So I may have to miss some class to keep up with these organizations but I promise to keep up.
